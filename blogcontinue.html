Sep 3, 2024

PetAg Hairball Solution Gel:

We all know a cat has to groom herself, but the downside? The hairballs. Bleh! A while ago, I started having fits of coughing with nothing coming out. Amelia took me to the vet and discovered that I had hairballs that were getting stuck inside me. Our veterinarian recommended a daily dose of brushing my fur and a hairball lubricant to help everything...uhhh...pass. As an average shedding rate cat, I must say that PetAg Hairball Solution has truly exceeded my expectations.

First, let’s address the taste: it's delightful! The flavor is so appealing that I actually look forward to my daily dose. It takes like a mixture of fried chicken and mapel syrup. YUM! You can see just how much I love it in the video below.

The texture is smooth and easy to lick off my human’s finger. But the real magic happens behind the scenes. Within just a few days, I noticed a significant decrease in those pesky hairballs that sometimes disrupt my grooming regimen. Now, I can strut around the house without the fear of an embarrassing hairball or coughing incident. My human appreciates the cat dying noise, and I appreciate the freedom to focus on more important matters, like napping in the sun or plotting my next treat heist.If you’re a cat who values comfort and style (and let’s be honest, who doesn’t?), I highly recommend PetAg Hairball Solution. It’s the purrfect addition to any kitty's routine!

Suction Cat Toy Wand:

I'm sure all felines can relate to the struggle of your humans going away for most of the day and not having anyone to play with you. That's why my humans bought me this toy. I must say that this suction cup cat toy has completely captured my attention! First off, the installation is effortless—just a quick smack on the floor, and voilà! It’s ready for action. It works for both tile and wood flooring. The toy spins and sways with the slightest touch, keeping me engaged in a delightful dance of pounces and swipes. I could play with it for hours and never get tired of it. In fact, I have. My sister, Bobbi, also really loves it.

It comes with three different feather attachments, and they are just right for a good chew when I’m feeling playful. I appreciate how it challenges my hunting instincts, making me feel like the fierce predator I am!

Best of all, I can entertain myself while my human is busy at college. It’s a win-win! I highly recommend this toy to my fellow feline friends and cat owners. The only downside is that the bottom of the suction part is quite sticky and stuff like hair has gotten stuck to it and it gets harder to stick on the ground over time. All I can say to that is try to maybe clean the floor area before you stick it down. Your humans will be impressed by your athletic prowess, and you’ll have endless fun!
